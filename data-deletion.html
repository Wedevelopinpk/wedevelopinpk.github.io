Data Deletion Request

WeDevelopInPK Apps — DataVault & Best DICOM Viewer

Overview

At WeDevelopInPK, we respect your privacy and ensure that our apps do not collect or store personal user data on our own servers. All user data is stored either locally on your device or through your chosen third-party cloud storage service (such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or WebDAV).

Since we do not operate any backend or online account system, there is no user data retained on our end. However, if you have ever contacted us for support or feedback, you can request deletion of that communication data.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data (such as support or feedback emails), please contact us at:

Email: wedevelopinpk@gmail.com

Please include the following details in your request:

  • Your name or the name used in correspondence
  • The app you are referring to (e.g., DataVault or Best DICOM Viewer)
  • The approximate date or subject of your email (if applicable)

We will confirm the deletion of your information within 7 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Support and feedback emails sent to wedevelopinpk@gmail.com
  • Any associated attachments or metadata in that correspondence
  • Temporary logs (if ever shared manually for troubleshooting)

What Data May Remain

  • Billing and payment records handled by Google Play — we do not have access to or control over these records.
  • Files or data stored in Google Drive, Dropbox, or WebDAV — users can delete these directly from their own accounts.
  • Anonymous analytics or advertising identifiers collected automatically by Google AdMob or Play Services, as described in our Privacy Policy.
