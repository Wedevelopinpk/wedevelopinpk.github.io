⚕️ Medical DICOM Viewer
Whether you are a medical professional, researcher, or student, Medical DICOM Viewer provides a fast, intuitive, and powerful platform to view and analyze DICOM (.dcm) files on your Android device.

📂 FLEXIBLE FILE & FOLDER ACCESS 📂
  • Single File Viewing: Quickly open individual DICOM files from your device.
  • Full Folder Support (Pro): Open an entire folder of DICOM studies and seamlessly navigate through them.
  • ZIP Archive Reading (Pro): Directly open and view DICOM files stored within .zip archives.
  • Google Drive™ Integration (Pro): Securely sign in to browse your own Google Drive, open shared folders with a link, and download files for offline viewing.
  • PACS Server Access (Pro): Connect to and browse DICOM files directly from PACS servers.
  • DICOM Library (Pro): Organize and manage your frequently used DICOM files in a local, persistent library.
🔬 ADVANCED IMAGE ANALYSIS TOOLS 🔬
  • Multi-Frame Playback: Animate and play through multi-frame sequences with intuitive playback controls.
  • Pan & Zoom: Effortlessly pan and zoom to examine fine details with smooth gesture controls.
  • Windowing Control: Adjust window center and width (WC/WW) to optimize image contrast and brightness, with common presets available.
  • Measurement Tool: Measure distances directly on the image in pixels or millimeters (if PixelSpacing tag is present).
  • Invert Colors: Instantly invert grayscale colors for alternative viewing.
  • Image Filters: Apply filters like Structure Enhancement, Edge Detection, or Stent Max for analysis.
  • Color Maps: Apply pseudo-color maps to grayscale images.
  • DICOM Tags Viewer: Inspect all embedded DICOM metadata.
✨ BUILT FOR A SEAMLESS EXPERIENCE ✨
  • Modern & Clean UI: A user-friendly interface that gets out of your way.
  • Persistent Locations: The app remembers your last-opened local and Google Drive folders for quick access.
  • Freemium Model: Core viewing is free with ads. A simple, one-time purchase unlocks all Pro features (Folder/ZIP/Drive/PACS/Library access) and removes ads.

Empower your medical imaging workflow. Download Medical DICOM Viewer today!

🔐 DataVault: Securely Hide Your Photos & Videos
Need a private space on your phone for personal photos, videos, or documents? DataVault provides a secure and easy-to-use vault to protect your sensitive media from prying eyes.

🛡️ STRONG SECURITY & ENCRYPTION 🛡️
  • Authenticated Encryption: Your files are protected using industry-standard authenticated encryption, verifying data integrity.
  • AES-256 Standard: We utilize the robust AES-256 standard for encryption. A hybrid approach ensures efficiency:
    • Files 5MB and smaller use AES-256-GCM.
    • Files larger than 5MB use Tink Streaming AEAD (AES256-GCM-HKDF-4KB) for optimized performance.
  • Secure Key Derivation: Your unique 256-bit encryption key is derived from your password using PBKDF2 with 65,536 iterations and a unique salt per file, resisting brute-force attacks.
  • Password/PIN Protection: Secure your vault with a primary PIN or password.
  • Offline Storage: Your encrypted files remain securely on your device unless you choose to use cloud sync.
🗂️ FILE MANAGEMENT FEATURES 🗂️
  • Hide Various File Types: Securely import photos, videos, audio, PDFs, and other file types.
  • In-App Viewing: View photos and play videos directly within the secure vault without exporting.
  • Batch Import/Export: Easily move multiple files in or out of the vault.
  • Organize Your Vault: Create, rename, and delete folders within the vault.
  • Sort Options: Sort files by name, date added, size, or type.
⭐ PRO FEATURES (Unlock via One-Time Purchase) ⭐
  • Ad-Free Experience: Enjoy DataVault without any advertisements.
  • Biometric Login: Use your device's fingerprint sensor for quick and secure access.
  • Cloud Sync: Optionally back up your encrypted vault to Google Drive, Dropbox, or WebDAV servers. Features a double-encryption model for enhanced cloud security (files are encrypted locally, then encrypted again before upload).
  • Multi-Password Support: Create multiple passwords for different vault access levels or contexts.
  • Gesture Controls: Enable convenient gestures, like shaking your device to lock the vault immediately.
✨ USER-FRIENDLY EXPERIENCE ✨
  • Clean Interface: Simple and intuitive design.
  • Progress Indicators: See visual progress during encryption/decryption.
  • Optional Original File Deletion: Choose to automatically delete original files from public storage after successful import.
