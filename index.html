🔐 DataVault: Your Ultimate Military-Grade Secure Vault
Tired of worrying about who might see your personal photos, private videos, and confidential documents?

DataVault isn't just another file hider — it’s a high-security digital safe built to protect your data with impenetrable, military-grade security protocols.

🛡️ IRONCLAD SECURITY & ENCRYPTION PROTOCOLS 🛡️
🔐 Military-Grade AES-256 Encryption: Every file you add is locked down with AES-256 encryption — trusted by governments worldwide.
🧠 PBKDF2 Key Derivation: Your encryption key is securely derived from your password using PBKDF2 with unique salt per file.
🔑 Android Keystore Protection: Your master password is separately encrypted using hardware-backed storage.
🕵️‍♂️ Stealth Folder Technology: Vault folders are disguised with random names — invisible to curious eyes.
📴 100% Offline: Your files are stored only on your device. You hold the only key.
